Your Privacy Matters

CreatiSphere’s mission is to connect the world’s Artists to allow them to be more productive and successful. Central to this mission is our commitment to be transparent about the data we collect about you, how it is used and with whom it is shared.

This Privacy Policy applies when you use our Services (described below). We offer our users choices about the data we collect, use and share as described in this Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy, Settings and our Help Center.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Data:

This is data that can identify you as an individual, such as your name, email address, profile picture, location, and social media accounts. We collect this data when you create an account, log in with a social media login button, update your profile, or contact us.

1.2 Content Data:

This is data that relates to your artwork, such as the title, description, tags, comments, likes, and views. We collect this data when you upload, edit, or delete your artwork, or when you interact with other users’ artwork.

1.3 Device Data:

This is data that relates to the device you use to access our platform, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, device model, and screen resolution. We collect this data automatically when you visit our website or app.

1.4 Third-party Data:

This is data that we receive from third-party sources, such as social media platforms, payment providers, analytics services, or advertising networks. We collect this data when you log in with a social media login button, make a purchase on our platform, or click on an advertisement.

2. How We Use Your Data:

We use your data to provide and improve our platform and its features, to personalize your user experience and show you relevant content, to communicate with you and respond to your inquiries, to facilitate transactions and process payments, to enforce our terms and conditions and protect our rights and safety, to comply with legal obligations and regulations, to measure and analyze our platform’s performance and usage, and to deliver targeted advertising and marketing campaigns1

We only use your data for the purposes that we have disclosed to you in our privacy policy. We do not sell, rent or lease your data to third parties without your consent. We respect your privacy and data rights, and we give you options and controls to manage your data on our platform

3. How We Share Information

  • Third Parties:We may share your personal information with third-party service providers that help us operate and improve our platform. These include hosting providers, analytics providers, payment processors, marketing partners, and security providers. We only share your personal information with third parties that respect your privacy and comply with applicable laws. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their own purposes.
  • Other User:We may share your personal information with other users of our platform when you interact with them through our features. For example, when you upload your artwork, comment on other users’ artwork, or send messages to other users. You can control what personal information you share with other users by adjusting your privacy settings.
  • Public Information:We may share your personal information with the public when you choose to make it visible on our platform. For example, when you create a public profile, post public artwork, or participate in public forums. You can control what personal information you make public by adjusting your privacy settings.
  • Law Enofcement or Required sharing:We may share your personal information with law enforcement authorities or other parties if we are required or permitted by law. For example, when we respond to a legal request, court order, or subpoena; when we protect our rights and safety or those of others; or when we cooperate with an investigation.

Your Choices and obligations

  • Accessing and Updating Your Personal: You can access and update most of your personal information on our platform through your account settings. For example, you can change your name, email address, profile picture, location, and social media accounts. If you want to access or update any other personal information that we have about you, you can contact us at privacy@creativeart.com.
  • Deleting Your Personal Information:You can delete some or all of your personal information on our platform by deleting your account. When you delete your account, we will also delete your artwork, comments, likes, messages, and any other content that you have created or shared on our platform. However, some of your personal information may remain on our platform for a limited period of time for legal or operational reasons. For example, we may retain some of your personal information to comply with tax laws, prevent fraud, resolve disputes, or enforce our terms and conditions.
  • Downloading Your Personal Information:ou can download a copy of your personal information that we have on our platform by requesting a data export. To request a data export, you can contact us at privacy@creativeart.com. We will provide you with a link to download a file containing your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawing Your Consent:You can withdraw your consent for us to collect, use, or share your personal information at any time by changing your privacy settings on our platform or contacting us at privacy@creativeart.com. However, withdrawing your consent may affect your ability to use some features or services on our platform. For example, if you withdraw your consent for us to use cookies or similar technologies, you may not be able to access some parts of our website or app.

Other important Information:

  • How we store and protect the data: We store the data that we collect from you on secure servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard security measures to protect the data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. These include encryption, firewalls, passwords, and regular backups. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.
  • Links to other policies on your website: Our privacy policy is part of our agreement with you when you use our platform or service. You should also read our other policies that are linked below before using our platform or service:
    1. Cookie Policy: This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website and app.
    2. Terms of Service: This policy sets out the rules and guidelines for using our platform or service.
    3. Refund Policy: This policy explains how we handle refunds and returns for purchases made on our platform or service.
  • How to access or remove data: You have the right to access, update, correct, or delete your personal data that we have on our platform or service. You can do so by logging into your account and changing your settings. If you want to request a copy of your data or delete your account entirely, you can contact us at privacy@creativeart.com.
  • How to contact us: If you have any questions or complaints about our privacy policy or practices, you can contact us at privacy@creativeart.com. You can also write to us at: CreativeArt Inc. 123 Main Street New York, NY 10001 USA
